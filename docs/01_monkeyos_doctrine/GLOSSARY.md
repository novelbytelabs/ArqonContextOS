- MonkeyOS:
  governed AI-team operating platform umbrella.
- ArqonMonkeyOS:
  current repository/platform name.
- ContextBus:
  repo-backed infrastructure layer for context, flow, artifacts, messages, and governance.
- ContextOS (legacy):
  legacy name for infrastructure now called ContextBus inside MonkeyOS.
- Science Monkeys:
  explore-side team for uncertain knowledge work.
- Code Monkeys:
  exploit-side team for product implementation from validated findings.
- Explore:
  investigate uncertainty through research, hypotheses, protocol design, execution, and audit.
- Exploit:
  convert validated findings into governed product development workflows.
- Share Packet:
  auditable bridge artifact from Science Monkeys to PM context.
- Finding Record:
  official science artifact capturing evidence level, claims allowed/forbidden, and review status.
- science_flow:
  flow family for scientific exploration lifecycle.
- code_flow:
  flow family for product development lifecycle.
- governance_flow:
  flow family for doctrine, policy, and process updates.
- Mike/Ash identity distinction:
  human identity is distinct from role identity; temporary labels may be used before OAuth.
