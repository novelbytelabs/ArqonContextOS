`science_flow` is the planned flow family for exploration work.

Planned lifecycle:

- `/research`
- `/hypothesize`
- `/design-experiment`
- `/execute-experiment`
- `/audit-experiment`
- `/interpret`
- `/iterate`
- `/record-finding`
- `/share`

Flow outputs:

- protocol artifacts
- execution evidence
- audit reports
- finding records
- share packets for PM context

Constraints:

- Evidence levels and claim boundaries are required.
- Share packets are context bridges, not promotion events.
